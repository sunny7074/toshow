Holidays

🔍 How to Use

Step 1: Select a country from the dropdown or type a 2-letter country code.

Step 2: Choose a year and optionally a month (or "All months").

Step 3: Click "Search Holidays" to load results. Holidays appear grouped by month as cards with details.

🎊 About This App

This app uses the Nager.Date public API to find holidays by country and year.
